Choose Your Loading Style

Whether front- or top-loading, all LG washers deliver high efficiency to reduce energy costs and water consumption (versus conventional washers), Plus, no matter which option you choose, you can count on the reliability of an LG Direct Drive motor.

FRONT LOAD

Front Load washers are the ultimate in energy and water efficiency. They are innovatively designed to offer deep cleaning on a variety of fabrics, including delicates, and are incredibly gentle on clothes.

TOP LOAD

LG's high efficiency top washers offer award-winning innovation in a traditional, convenient top-load style. Featuring LG's largest available capacity, these washers can tackle more laundry in fewer loads.
